# H-Note Classroom: The 3-Stage Professional Blueprint

## Stage 1: Talent Development (The Foundation)
* **Class 1: Music & Vocals** – Finding your professional frequency and vocal signature.
* **Class 2: Dance & Performing** – Mastering physical presence, spatial command, and stage authority.
* **Class 3: Writing & Visual Art** – Developing the elemental tools of creative narrative and design.

## Stage 2: Strategy Integration (The Bridge)
* **Class 1: Music & Vocals** – Translating rhythm and tone into **Sales Psychology and Negotiation**.
* **Class 2: Dance & Performing** – Applying stage presence to **Leadership and Boardroom Command**.
* **Class 3: Writing & Visual Art** – Converting creative prose into **Strategic Marketing and Brand Copy**.

## Stage 1-2 Note:
*Stages 1 and 2 utilize the same artistic disciplines to build muscle memory, transitioning the focus from pure creative expression to professional business application.*

## Stage 3: The Launch (The Business Model)
* **The Master Class: Market Execution** – Integrating all disciplines from the previous stages into a single, scalable business model ready for a live market launch.
